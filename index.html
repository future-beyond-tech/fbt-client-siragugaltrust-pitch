05 — Donor Experience

Donate the way you want. Once. Monthly. Or sponsor a life.

Today, the Siragugal team chases monthly donations on WhatsApp, types receipts by hand, reconciles bank statements in Excel, and writes year-end statements in Google Docs. V2 ends all of that — and gives donors three new ways to give that the current site can't even ask for.

PILLAR · 01

Give the way that fits

Five flexible patterns · one branded checkout

  • One-time donationUPI · cards · netbanking · wallets · 30-second flow
  • Monthly recurringRazorpay subscription · auto-debit · pause anytime
  • Annual givingSingle payment · marked for tax year · auto 80G
  • Pay-by-intent tiers"₹500 = school supplies for a child" · impact card before checkout
  • Micro-tip optionRound-up donations · ₹100 / ₹250 / ₹500 quick buttons
  • NRI giving (Phase 2)Stripe rail for USD · GBP · AED · SGD donations
  • Branded checkoutNever leaves the siragugaltrust domain
PILLAR · 02

The end of manual billing

Webhooks · mandates · auto-receipts · zero ops work

  • Razorpay eMandate / NACHDonor authorises once · charged forever (until they pause)
  • Pre-charge remindersSMS + WhatsApp + email · 3 days before debit
  • Webhook-driven recordingEvery payment auto-logged to DB in real time
  • Auto 80G receipt PDFGenerated at success · emailed · stored in donor portal
  • Failed-payment dunningSmart 3-retry schedule before gentle pause
  • Auto-resume on card updateDonor updates card · recurring resumes silently
  • Daily reconciliationBank statement ↔ donations auto-matched · CSV export
  • Annual giving statementAuto-generated and emailed every March
  • Refund flowAdmin-triggered · fully audited · edge case but supported

06 — Engineering

The stack — chosen for longevity, not novelty.

A trust runs for decades. The website should too. FBT picks tools that compound maintainability and recruit-ability over time, not the framework of the month.

Frontend

PPR rendering · server components · the React 19 baseline. Fast everywhere, indexable everywhere.

Custom JWT + OTP
Donor accounts · admin roles · OTP-before-persistence. No third-party lock-in.

Plausible
No cookies · GDPR-clean · public dashboard option for transparency.

07 — Discoverability

Found by humans. Found by AI. Found by voice.

In 2026, donors don't only Google their causes — they ask Perplexity, ChatGPT Search, Gemini, and Alexa. FBT's V2 build optimizes Siragugal across all three discovery surfaces: classical SEO, Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). The result: when someone in Tamil Nadu asks "trusted education NGOs in Sivagangai district," Siragugal shows up — wherever they're asking.

PILLAR · 01

Classical SEO

Google · Bing · DuckDuckGo

Indexable, fast, structured. The fundamentals, done right.

  • Schema.org NGO markupNGO · NonProfit · Organization JSON-LD on every page
  • Sitemap.xml + robots.txtAuto-generated · submitted to Google Search Console
  • OpenGraph + Twitter Card metaEvery page renders properly when shared
  • Semantic HTML5<article> · <section> · <nav> · proper heading hierarchy
  • Core Web Vitals targetsLCP < 2.5s · CLS = 0 · INP < 200ms · FCP < 1.5s
  • Image SEONext.js Image · alt text · WebP/AVIF · responsive srcset
  • Internal linking architecturePrograms ↔ stories ↔ campaigns cross-linked
  • Local SEOTamil Nadu · Sivagangai · Manamadurai geo-tags · Google Business Profile
PILLAR · 02

AI Search-Ready

Perplexity · ChatGPT Search · Gemini · Claude

Content shaped to be quoted, cited, and summarized by LLMs.

  • LLM-readable content structureClear claim · evidence · citation pattern
  • llms.txt fileDeclares mission · programs · key facts · contact for AI crawlers
  • Citation-friendly fact pagesEvery program has a "By the Numbers" block
  • Authoritative tone in copyDirect claims with supporting data · no hedge words
  • Entity disambiguationSivagangai Siragugal Trust ≠ other "Siragugal" entities
  • FAQPage JSON-LDFor the most-asked donor questions
  • Beneficiary stories with structured dataPerson · Article · dated · consented
  • Quote-ready summariesEvery page has a 2–3 sentence "TL;DR" block
PILLAR · 03

Voice & Featured Snippets

Google Assistant · Alexa · Siri · featured boxes

Structured to answer questions in 40 words or less.

  • HowTo schemaFor "how to donate to Siragugal"-type queries
  • Question / Answer schemaOn every FAQ entry
  • Conversational long-tail content"Where does my donation go?"-style pages
  • Featured-snippet optimized blocksDefinition · steps · table format
  • Voice-search-friendly phrasingNatural questions in headings
  • Speakable schemaMarks sections suitable for voice readout
  • Local-question pages"NGOs near Manamadurai" · "Tamil Nadu education trusts"
  • Bilingual answer enginesTamil + English questions both indexed
